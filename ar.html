Ada's Augmented Seeing Aparatus

Ada has done it again! She has invented an augmented seeing aparatus that will help you fix those pesky Music Machines that keep breaking down in all the neighborhoods. To get started, follow Ada's instructions to print the secret image marker that makes the aparatus work. Without it, your detective arsenal is incomplete!

  1. Download and print the pdf
  2. Place the target image face-up on a flat surface.
  3. Launch the Wollstonecraft Detective Agency app and tap on a Music Machine in any neighbourhood to open the AR activity.
  4. Point the device camera at the target image. The music machine will appear on your screen.

 

HINT: You can also point your device at another screen! Tell your other detective friend to show the image on their tablet.
