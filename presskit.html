Description

The Wollstonecraft Detective Agency is an exploration-based puzzle adventure game set in the early 19th century that features Ada Lovelace, the world’s first computer programmer, and Mary Shelley, the world’s first science fiction author. Based on the acclaimed book series by Jordan Stratford, the game celebrates famous female role models and promotes the learning of science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM). Distributed as a freemium game for tablets and smartphones, players are tasked with helping Ada and Mary through a series of puzzles, games, and challenges as they help solve mysteries for the detective agency.

The sleuths explore Regency-era neighborhoods of London, full of curious and whimsical characters and a steampunk feel. They learn facts about a fascinating time in history at the tail end of the industrial revolution, when numerous technological innovations occurred. They encounter Charles Babbage (creator of the first mechanical computer), Charles Dickens (famous author), and many other fascinating characters of the time.

With rich dialogue and engaging storylines, players join Ada and Mary’s clandestine detective agency and help the girls solve each mystery. This involves cracking codes and mastering puzzles, some simple programming, as well as regular old detective work, prowling the streets in search of informants that might offer the next clue.

The Rube-Goldberg-inspired world of Wollstonecraft is frequently funny and playful, odd and antique. Historical locations come to life as animated, interactive 3D scenes, ranging from residential neighborhoods to commercial streets and down to the dockyards. Each location can be explored with point-and-click style interaction, with hidden objects to discover, and puzzles to solve. Clues are collected in the detective’s log book, where achievements are tracked and further reference material can be found. Augmented reality features further expand the detective’s arsenal, providing additional gadgetry to explore.

The Wollstonecraft Detective Agency inspires kids to explore science and discovery through STEM-based educational themes. The narrative is filled with humor, adventure, and a strong female cast. It is a premium experience for parents, educators, and kids of all ages to enjoy.

History

The Wollstonecraft Detective Agency began as a book series in 2012 with a record-setting Kickstarter campaign for children’s literature. Supporters rallied to the series’ values that celebrated girls in STEM (science, technology, engineering and math) and provided real-life historic role models. Written by Jordan Stratford, the series has subsequently published 4 books with Penguin Random House, and more are on the way!

Stratford partnered with Hololabs in 2016 to create an exploration-based puzzle adventure game to accompany the book series. With funding from the Canada Media Fund, the game features the same wonderful cast of characters, a multitude of puzzles, and of course, that same intrigue and adventure of the book series.

About Hololabs

Founded in 2011 by researchers in intelligent systems and veterans from AAA gaming, Hololabs fuses creative interaction design with advanced engineering to create innovative and engaging new media experiences. With a portfolio ranging from interactive physical installations to mobile games and virtual reality, the company has collaborated with many renowned artists, participated in international festivals and conferences, and has won international awards for its projects.
