Privacy Policy

General

The Wollstonecraft Detective Agency website and mobile application are developed by Hololabs Studio Inc.. At Hololabs, we take privacy seriously and this policy is designed to share what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

In order to improve our product and provide a better experience, we collect anonymous information such as events completed and actions taken within the game. This data is collected using a third-party service provided by Unity Technologies. More information is provided within the game using the "Data Privacy" button in the Settings Menu, where you can view what information was collected by Unity and request that they remove this data from their server. More details about Unity’s Privacy Policy can be found here: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

For parents who subscribe to our newsletter, we do collect personally identifiable information such as name and email addresses, but these are strictly used only for that purpose and are not shared with any third parties. The newsletter is not intended for children under the age of 13. All data related to the newsletter is managed by our mailing list service provider, MailChimp. You can read MailChimp’s privacy policy here: https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/

We do not automatically collect personal information through our website(s).

Your Access to and Control Over Information

You may ask us to delete any information about you at any time by contacting us via the contact email address on this website. To delete data collected by the game, use the "Data Privacy" button in the Settings Menu and follow the dialogue.

Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act

Our mobile applications(s) comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (“COPPA”). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 years old. If a user is identified as under 13 years old, we will not collect or use any information of this user and we will delete any information already received in a secure manner.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information for marketing purposes, but anonymous information may be shared with third parties to help us run and maintain our services.

Security

We take precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline. Wherever we collect sensitive information, that information is encrypted and transmitted in a secure way.

Updates

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time as we add new or different features, or as the law changes. All updates will be posted on this page and unless another date is given, the changes are effective upon posting. If we make a material change to this policy we will post notice here before making the change and will use whatever other means we can (such as the newsletter) to notify you about the changes.

This privacy policy was last updated on March 1, 2020.

Questions

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, contact us by email at info@hololabs.org.
